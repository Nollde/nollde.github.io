Selected Papers

FlexCAST: Enabling Flexible Scientific Data Analyses (Jul 2025)

Have you ever wondered how to unlock the full potential of your scientific data analyses in the long run? FlexCAST revolutionizes the preservation of data analysis by preserving the analysis design in a modular way, allowing for changes to input data and parametrization, and ensuring validity and robustness.

Stay Positive: Neural Refinement of Sample Weights (Jun 2025)

Tired of dealing with negative weights in simulated physics events? Our neural refinement method solves the challenge by utilizing a phase space-dependent factor to refine initial simulation weights and helps you to always stay positive!

Anomaly Detection on LHC Data (Feb 2025)

What if you could detect anomalies in high-energy physics data without being tied to a specific signal model? Our latest anomaly detection search uses machine learning strategies to search for potential excesses in LHC data in a weakly supervised way.
